Search Engine Optimization and Keyword Research

At Hamline I am responsible for search engine optimization and keyword research. There are four simple tactics that get you 80% of the way to full optimization:

  • URL structure
  • Page title
  • Header tags
  • Keyword metadata

The key is to make sure that the right keywords appear in those four areas on the specific, related page on your site. Don't spam your page with your keywords! Use them in those four areas and in your page content sparingly and you'll make huge strides in your search engine optimization. And how do you know what keywords you should use? Keyword research is the foundation to proper search engine optimization -- make sure you use a tool like Google Keywords to see what phrases potential visitors use.

Analysis

The most important component of a digital marketing campaign isn't the design, writing, or particular channel used: it's the resulting data. A solid grasp of web traffic data is essential when you're putting money into various tactics; it helps drive smart adjustments to a campaign, evaluate a channel or tactic, and increase marketing efficiency.

I am a proficient web data analyst with experience in:

  • Setting up goals and analyzing goal conversions
  • Tracking campaigns across channels
  • Using web traffic data to measure success of a redesign
  • Multivariate (A/B) testing
  • Social media analytics, including which indicators to obsess over and which to ignore
  • Email open and click-through rates
  • Using the above to make strategic decisions about websites and campaigns
