What can I do for you?

Design

First, it's most important to identify what your goals are. Then, we move into the nitty of what you were thinking. From wireframes (aka pre-design, to Photoshop and other Adobe products, to optimizing images, and everything in between. In the web world these days, "designers" aren't often "developers".

Coding

HTML/CSS, HTML5/CSS3 (yep, all the fancy stuff), LESS, JQuery plugins, content management systems, installing Wordpress, uploading final files to your hosting service. Does it need to be responsive? I can do that. Do you need to be able to update it yourself? Of course you do! I can help with that as well.

Usability

Usability (or user experience) ensures that visitors (potential customers) have a positive, easy experience with your site. It's not just if they can do what you want them to do... can they do what they are looking to do? Navigation, site structure, functionality (clicks), wayfinding, user tasks, etc.

Strategy

Not sure where to start, or what your website should include/look like/do? Let me help. I can help you distill what your goals are and translate those into the components you need. My strategy work also includes market research, best practices, target demographics, opposition research, and cost/benefit analysis.

Worried about cost?

Don't be. Gone are the days where you need to spend thousands of dollars to get a high-quality site. There are literally hundreds, if not thousands, of tools that help keep time (and therefore cost) to a mimimum. Throw your number at me and let's see what we can do!

Content

Content strategy is the next big buzzword in the web world. Are you meeting your users' needs? How do you connect your social strategy with your web strategy to drive conversions? I also partner with the best copywriters in the business.

SEO/SEM

Keyword research, making sure your page titles/headers/description are up to par, link checks. SEM is search engine marketing: Google Adwords, PPC, display ads (design and strategy), landing pages, conversion.

Social

Need help with your social media? From posting strategy, to growing engagement, to understanding what metrics to pay attention to (and which are meaningless). I can also help with starting and monitoring social ad campaigns.

Analytics

Heavily experienced with Google Analytics. How do you know if your website is successful? By setting goals. This is key for all websites, not just e-commerce. Keep track of important metrics and see how they improve over time.
