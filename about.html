About Sarah

Hello all! I'm a web and user experience designer, digital marketer, photographer, and designer living in Minneapolis, MN. A Coloradan by birth, I landed in Minnesota for college and immediately declared as an art major, ignoring Mom's handwringing and some very logical advice that something more practical would be a better investment.

After college, I was confident that web design would best pair with my design-focused right brain and structure/strategy-focused left brain. I taught myself how to hand-code HTML and CSS with the help of a great book and completed a master's program that gave me a strong theoretical background in communication. Several years later and multiple jobs later, here I am: fully immersed in digital life!
