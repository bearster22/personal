School of Business MBA

I designed and implemented a major redesign of the MBA site's navigation and content. Conceptualized and strategized an MBA-specific left menu, with sections for degree info, tuition and financial aid, and admission. Swapped the old text-heavy template with a rotating banner template, creating more high-profile opportunities to market specific elements of the program. Highlighted content on the homepage that appeared frequently in internal search data: tuition, assistantships, program schedule. Incorporated graduate testimonials and embedded videos of former students and faculty talking about the benefits of the program.
